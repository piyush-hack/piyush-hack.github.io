What Are Chunks

×
Chunks Are The No Of Images You Want To Contain In A Single Image.
Suppose Chunks Are 70 and you selected 100 images then there will be 2 download buttons one with first 70 images and other with next 30 images.
70 and 8 are the recommended chunks in vertical and horizontal merging respectively. If selected a few times larger chunks then it may show error since width of images may become very small then the minium width.

What Is Process Time

×
Its the Time Unit Given To Process The Lining Of Images .
This matter only when large no of images like above 50 are selected.
Suppose You Have Selected 100 images with 70 chunks and process time is kept at 0 then images may be added in a random manner.
But If procces time is 1000 then images will be in selected manner.
In case of maore images like 160 with chunks 70 first 70- 80 images will be in slected manner but the in random . IN this case upload images in 2 or mutiple times (they will be added to bottom previously added images)

These Buttons Doesn't zoom in or out.
These are for changing width because images may blur out or may not be very clear if not set to their appropriate width .
One can use them to zoom but remember to set it to approiate width before downloading otherwise images will be downloaded in the wodth you have set.

Upload Images

×
You Can Upload as many images as you want at a time.
Now suppose you uploaded 10 images at one time and then added 7 more images to it.
Now there will be 2 download buttons at the bottom . One for yhe first 10 images and other one for next 7 i.e. every time you uploaded images a button will be created for that upload .
New images will not be downloaded with the same button with which previously added images were being downloaded
